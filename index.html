FAQs

How does URL shortening work?
URL shortening works by taking a long URL and creating a shorter, condensed version that redirects to the original URL. When a user clicks on the shortened link, they are redirected to the intended destination.
Is it necessary to create an account to use the URL shortening service?
While you can use basic URL shortening features without an account, creating one gives you access to additional features like link analytics, custom URLs, and link management.
Are the shortened links permanent? Will they expire?
Our shortened links are permanent and will not expire as long as they comply with our terms of service and are actively used within a reasonable timeframe.
Are there any limitations on the number of URLs I can shorten?
Free users have a monthly limit, while premium users get unlimited URL shortening capabilities along with additional features.
Can I customize the shortened URLs to reflect my brand or content?
Yes, premium users can create custom branded links that include their company name or relevant keywords for better brand recognition.
Can I track the performance of my shortened URLs?
Yes, we provide detailed analytics including click rates, geographic data, and device information for all shortened URLs.
How secure is the URL shortening service? Are the shortened links protected against spam or malicious activity?
We employ multiple security measures including link scanning, spam detection, and user reporting to ensure the safety of our shortened links.
What is a QR code and what can it do?
A QR code is a two-dimensional barcode that can be scanned by smartphones to quickly access websites, text, or other digital content.
Is there an API available for integrating the URL shortening service into my own applications or websites?
Yes, we offer a comprehensive API that allows developers to integrate our URL shortening service into their applications and websites.
